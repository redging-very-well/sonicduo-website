Sonic Duo

Music for any occasion

Welcome to Sonic Duo,
a fantastic choice for
your next event!

With the rhythmic and percussive elements of acoustic guitar combined with the ambience and richness of electric guitar, we create a unique sound that is sure to impress. Both accomplished vocalists, we cover a wide range of genres!

Whether you're looking for a lively performance to get the crowd dancing or a mellow background ambient experience, we've got the repertoire to suit. If you're after something a bit more bespoke, we also have a growing collection of our very own original compositions. You can often find us playing the open mic circuit honing our skills; a great way to come and see us play for free!

We're available to play your next event; check out some of our clips below and get in touch with us today!

Whenuapai Community

Community event/background music

Sonic Duo got a lot of love at the Whenuapai Community event, where we played a mix of original songs and covers. The crowd was fantastic, and we had a great time performing for everyone. We played a range of genres, from classic rock to modern pop, and even a few originals. We received a lot of positive feedback, and it was great to see so many people enjoying our music. We also had the opportunity to connect with local musicians and other artists - a highlight of the event for us!

Hobsonville Artpoint

Community event performance

Sonic Duo joined the line up at Hobsonville Artpoint, a community event that celebrates local artists and musicians. We played a mix of original songs and covers to an awesome crowd of local people at the rifle range. The atmosphere was electric, and we had a great time performing for everyone. We got to play with many other talented musicians and artists, including a huge ensemble at the end of the day. It was great to see so many people coming together to support the local arts community.
